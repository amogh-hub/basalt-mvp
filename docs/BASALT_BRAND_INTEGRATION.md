# Official Basalt Brand Integration

Phase 6 integrates the founder-provided Basalt wordmark into the Command Center.

## Logo system

- `basalt-wordmark-mask.png` — reusable monochrome geometry controlled by CSS `currentColor`
- `basalt-wordmark-dark.png` — soft off-white wordmark for obsidian surfaces
- `basalt-wordmark-light.png` — near-black wordmark for light surfaces
- `basalt-mark-dark.png` and `basalt-mark-light.png` — compact app marks

## Locked behavior

- the wordmark geometry does not change between themes
- dark mode is the primary Basalt experience
- no lime or neon brand accent
- no glow, gradient fill, mascot, emoji, or cartoon treatment
- minimum contrast and clear-space rules should be preserved by consuming surfaces

## Product character

**Stealth luxury + engineering precision + dark operational control.**
